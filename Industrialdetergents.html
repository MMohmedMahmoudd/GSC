Industrial Detergents

At GSC, our Industrial Detergents are crafted to tackle the toughest cleaning and maintenance challenges in industrial environments. Designed for heavy-duty applications, these detergents ensure maximum cleanliness, safety, and operational efficiency across various sectors, including oil and gas, manufacturing, and construction.Our Industrial Detergents cover essential applications to support clean and safe operations:

DEGREASOL AM

An all-purpose degreaser for removing stubborn grease and contaminants in industrial settings.

Degrasol 1090 Series

is a powerful solvent-based degreaser with excellent cleaning and dissolving capacity, ideal for cleaning oil tanks, machines, engines, spare parts, and carriage floors.

ELECTRO CLEANER

Effective cleaner for electrical equipment, ensuring safe removal of oils and dirt without damaging sensitive components.

TENOL CLEANER 410

Specialized for industrial and machinery cleaning, providing efficient dirt and residue removal.

ALKALINE FOAM CLEANER

An effective foam cleaner for industrial surfaces, providing powerful degreasing with easy application.

Cooler Cleaner 1090

Cleans coolers and heat exchangers, ensuring effective cooling system maintenance.

Glob Remover 1090

A versatile cleaner for industrial applications, removing tough dirt and contaminants.

SUPER DEGREASER CONC

A high-performance degreaser for rigorous industrial cleaning tasks, tackling tough grease and grime.

CARBON REMOVER

Formulated to eliminate carbon deposits from machinery, engines, and industrial surfaces.

RUST REMOVER

Removes rust and corrosion from metal surfaces, restoring equipment and extending its lifespan.

Sludge Remover

Designed to dissolve and remove sludge buildup in tanks, pipes, and industrial equipment

Our Industrial Detergents offer reliable, high-performance solutions tailored to meet the rigorous demands of industrial cleaning. Formulated for durability and safety, GSC’s detergents are essential for maintaining cleanliness and operational efficiency in high-demand environments.

About Us

Established in 2012, Global Specialty Chemicals (GSC) has become a leading supplier of industrial chemical solutions

proudly serving a broad range of sectors in Egypt and across the region. Over the years, we have earned a reputation for delivering exceptional quality, reliability, and innovation in products such as detergents, oil & grease removers, solvents, thinners, oilfield chemicals, plastics, and printing solvents
